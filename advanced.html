Latest Developments & Research Areas

Multimodal AI

Models like Google's Gemini are pushing boundaries by understanding and operating across text, images, audio, video, and code simultaneously. This allows for more nuanced and human-like interaction and problem-solving.

AI Agents

Beyond answering prompts, AI agents can take action. They can autonomously browse the web, write and execute code, and perform multi-step tasks to achieve a goal, showing early signs of digital autonomy.

Generative Physical AI

This involves training AI models to control robots in the physical world. Companies are developing models that can learn from video and text to perform complex, real-world tasks, from sorting objects to assisting in manufacturing.
